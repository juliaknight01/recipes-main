Waffles

These are the BEST Waffles! They are crispy, so fluffy, and very simple with only pantry ingredients! If you're simply seeking a delightful twist to your breakfast routine, these waffles are a delicious revelation that promises to make your mornings memorable.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

15 min.

servings

6 servings

Tags: Breakfast Lunch Dinner Desserts

instructions

Step 1

Mix the ingredients in a large bowl.

Step 2

Turn on and preheat your waffle maker.

Step 3

Remove each waffle and place on a baking sheet in your oven or toaster oven on "warm" or 200F/95C to keep warm and crispy until finished. Enjoy!

Ingredients

1 1/2 cups unsweetened soy milk

2 cups all-purpose flour

1 tablespoon brown sugar

1/2 teaspoon salt

2 teaspoons apple cider vinegar

1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

1/4 cup melted coconut oil

1 tablespoon baking powder
