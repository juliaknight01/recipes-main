Burrito

These Burritos are packed with a super easy walnut meat, beans, rice, salsa and guac and are loaded with flavour! They are great for meal prep, are comforting and are great for lunches or dinners.

prep time

25 min.

cook time

20 min.

servings

8 servings

Tags: Breakfast Lunch Dinner Desserts

instructions

Step 1

In a food processor, add the drained walnuts, 1 tablespoon of oil and the 2 tablespoons of water.

Step 2

In a pan over medium heat, add the last tablespoon of oil. When hot, add in the onion and garlic and saute for about 5 minutes. Add in the walnut meat, stir to combine, and then add in the soy sauce, liquid smoke, chili powder, garlic powder, paprika, cumin, sea salt and ground black pepper. Add in the black beans, stir, and allow to saute for another 5 minutes.

Step 3

In a separate bowl, add the tomato paste/sauce, paprika, salt and pepper to the warm rice. Stir to combine and set aside.

Step 4

Heat back up the pan over medium heat and lightly oil. Back in the pan, place the burrito seam down, and let it cook for about 2 to 3 minutes until the wrap is fully sealed and the burrito is golden brown. Flip, and do the other side for 2 minutes. Enjoy!

Ingredients

1/2 medium onion, diced

1 cup raw walnuts, soaked overnight or in boiling water for 10 minutes

3 cloves garlic, peeled and minced

1 1/2 cups cooked white or brown rice

8 large burrito wraps

2 cups lettuce

1/2 teaspoon chili powder

1 1/2 cups cooked black beans

1/2 teaspoon soy sauce

1 cup guacamole

1 cup salsa
