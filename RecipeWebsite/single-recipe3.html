Apple Pie

With a mountain of cinnamon apples nestled under a perfectly flaky pie crust, this is most certainly my favorite apple pie recipe. This pie has soft apples, baked with fragrant cinnamon, encased in flakey pastry. Perfect for Thanksgiving or Christmas!

prep time

20 min.

cook time

55 min.

servings

6 servings

Tags: Breakfast Lunch Dinner Desserts

instructions

Step 1

Place the apple slices and salt in a large bowl and microwave for 5 minutes. Mix, then microwave for another 5 minutes. Drain off any liquid released from the apples. Mix in the brown sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, cinnamon, and ginger with the apples. Set aside to cool.

Step 2

Place 1 ball of dough onto a large piece of lightly floured parchment paper. Sprinkle with some flour. Place the second piece of parchment over top and use a rolling pin to roll it into a ⅛ inch (¼ cm) thick circle, or about 4 cm wider than your pie plate. Repeat with the second ball of dough.

Step 3

When ready to assemble, preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C). Peel off a parchment sheet from the dough and flip it into a 9-inch (23 cm) glass pie plate so that the second parchment sheet is facing up. Enjoy!

Ingredients

35 oz apples

3 Tbsp brown sugar

1 tsp ground cinnamon

2½ cups all-purpose flour

1 Tbsp granulated sugar

4 Tbsp water

¼ tsp salt

½ lemon, juiced
