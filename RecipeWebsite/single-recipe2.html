White Bean Soup

This white bean soup is made in one pot, ready in 30 minutes and perfect for meal prep! It’s perfect with some rustic bread for dipping!

prep time

15 min.

cook time

30 min.

servings

6 servings

Tags: Breakfast Lunch Dinner Desserts

instructions

Step 1

Saute the finely chopped onion in a large pot. Once it starts to brown slightly, add in the garlic, celery and carrot. Saute an additional 10 or so minutes to let the veggies soften and brown slightly.

Step 2

Add in all remaining ingredients except for the kale.

Step 3

Bring to a boil, then cover and reduce heat to low. Let simmer for 15 minutes. Add in the chopped kale and let simmer for a few minutes to allow the kale to wilt.

Step 4

Taste and adjust flavors as desired. Serve warm as is or with a hearty bread on the side. Enjoy!

Ingredients

3 15 ounce cans cannellini beans

1 yellow onion

2 large carrots

2 cups chopped kal

4 cups vegetable broth

1 tbsp tomato paste

1 tsp salt

½ tsp dried oregano

2 bay leaves

1 tsp dried thyme
